What Is Black Clover?

Overview

In a world where magic and status are everything, Yuno and Asta aim to become one of the most influential figures in the country known as the Wizard King. However, the odds are stacked against them as not only are they peasants, but Asta can't even use magic. that being said, he did work out and train his body to the extreme everyday to make up for it. When it is time for the pair to receive their grimoires, the source of every wizards power, Yuno shocks everyone by receiving a rare grimoire with a four leaf clover rather than the typical three leaf. Asta, on the other hand, doesn't even receive a grimoire.

But when Yuno is attacked by someone jealous that he got a four leaf, Asta steps in to save his childhood friend. As expected, even with Asta's stength, he was no match for the wizard he was up against. As the wizard talked down on Asta for having no magic, Yuno came to his defense reminding Asta that his magic was "never giving up". With his childhood friend and rival's encouragement, a black grimoire with a five leaf clover appears before Asta. A black sword comes out of it that Asta can only wield because of his intense training. It has the ability to cut through and nullify magic and he uses it to beat the bad guy and free Yuno.

After receiving their grimoires, the pair set out to the capital of the Clover Kingdom to try and take the first step to becoming the Wizard King, joining a Magic Knight squad. The trials set to test the potential recruits were a breeze for Yuno who was always a talented wizard, but Asta struggled to accomplish any of the tasks. By the end, the only task Asta had successfully completed was the combat trial where not only Asta's rare anti-magic sword gave him an advantage, but his opponent also underestimated him. When it came time for the squad captains to choose who they wanted for their teams, Yuno was selected by all of them before ultimately choosing to join the top team, the Golden Dawn. Asta, however, wasn't selected by any of them. Until Yami Sukehiro, leader of the notoriously bad Black Bulls, accepts him into the squad. Thus Asta's journey to becoming the Wizard King begins!

A Snippet of the World of Black Clover

Black Clover takes place in the Clover Kingdom. Surrounding the Clover Kingdom are the Spade, Heart, and Club kingdoms. While not much is known about the Club Kingdom, the Heart Kingdom is in tune with natural magic and the Diamond Kingdom is a militaristic kingdom where conquest is everything. The Clover Kingdom is rife with class discrimination and discrimination against those with low level magic. Within the Clover Kingdom, it is said that in the three leaves lie faith, hope, and love. In a fourth leaf like Yuno's, lies luck. In a fifth like Asta's, lies a demon.

What I Like About Black Clover

Black Clover has many things that I enjoy. Magic, comedy, but most importantly, great characters. When I'm not laughing at Asta's single-minded pursuit of a nun he can't actually marry because of her oaths or cheering him on due to his determination, I'm taken in by the other members of the Black Bulls. Gordon looks creepy and mumbles hwen he speaks but just wants to be friends with everyone. Charmy's ravenous appetite is in stark contrast to her miniature size and has a wonderfully mascotish personality. All of the characters, especially in the Black Bulls, are quirky and seem like the kind of people that you would wanna hang with in the real world.
