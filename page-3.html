My Top 5 Favorite Characters

These are a the top 5 characters from Black Clover in my opinion. (If your list doesn't match though, you're just wrong.) Click on their names to learn more.

  • Yami Sukehiro- The rough and boorish leader of the Black Bulls who always inspires his team to do their best and beyond.
  • Mimosa Vermillion- Despite her status as a royal, Mimosa is a ray of sunshine and views everyone equally. Well, everyone but Asta, who she seems to have a crush on, and her brother, who she hates.
  • Julius Novachrono- The 28th Wizard King and beloved by the people, even moreso than the actual king, Julius seeks to make the Clover Kingdom a place where status doesn't matter and all are viewed as equals. He is also a huge magic nerd.
  • Asta- The magicless muscle-head that never gives up. When his back is to the wall, he surpasses his limits and gets the job done. Although he is very loud, oblivious, and sometimes downright moronic.
  • Charmy Pappitson- The GOAT!!! This food loving lady with magic as powerful as her appetite could rule the world if she chooses. But she would rather spend her time making delicious food for her friends and fawning over her food saving prince, Yuno.
